Trading and Technical Analysis Course

A comprehensive program designed for investment professionals and CMT exam preparation

Program Overview

The technical analysis and trading program is specially designed for investment professionals and those preparing for the Chartered Market Technician (CMT) exam, provided by the Market Technicians Association (MTA), USA.

Duration

42-45 hours total

Schedule

3 hours per day, Saturdays and Sundays

Levels

3 comprehensive levels

Target Audience

  • Traders
  • Investment Managers
  • Financial Brokers
  • Investors
  • Capital Market Professionals
  • Fund Managers
  • Financial Consultants
  • Investment Analysts
  • Academics
  • Stock and Derivative Dealers

Course Structure

Level I: Foundation

Learning Objectives:

  • Recognize types of charts
  • Identify support/resistance levels
  • Interpret price/volume characteristics
  • Understand pattern recognition
  • Study technical theories
  • Mass Psychology
  • Benefits of Moving Averages

Week 1 Topics:

  • Market History & Theories
  • Dow Theory & Charts
  • Technical vs. Fundamental
  • Support & Resistance
  • Trends & Patterns
  • Moving Averages

Level II: Advanced Concepts

Learning Objectives:

  • Utilizing long term charts
  • Identifying trend channels
  • Momentum oscillators
  • Japanese Candlestick Patterns
  • False signals handling
  • Relative Strength
  • Fibonacci Ratios

Week 2 & 3 Topics:

  • Technical Indicators
  • Arbitrage Strategies
  • Hedging and Speculation
  • Advanced Pattern Analysis

Level III: Expert Techniques

Learning Objectives:

  • Interest rates impact
  • Intermarket Analysis
  • Elliott Wave Analysis
  • Option strategies
  • Money management
  • Trading tactics

Week 4-7 Topics:

  • Advanced Market Analysis
  • Complex Trading Strategies
  • Risk Management
  • Structured Products
